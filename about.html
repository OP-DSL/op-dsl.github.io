If you have general questions, ideas, please feel free to email Istvan or Gihan, or join our mailing list here

If you are interested in the development of OP2 or OPS, get in touch with us - we are also on HipChat, for development related discussion. To join, let us know and we will send an invitation.

Current project leads

Gihan Mudalige is an Associate Professor at the Department of Computer Science, University of Warwick.

Istvan Reguly is an Assistant Professor at the Faculty of Information Technology and Bionics, Pazmany Peter Catholic Univeristy.

Previous project leads

Mike Giles is a Professor of Scientific Computing in the Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford.

Paul H. J. Kelly is a Professor of Software Technology in the Department of Computing, Imperial College London.
